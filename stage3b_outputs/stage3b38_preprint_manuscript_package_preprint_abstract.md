# Preprint abstract draft

We present an author-led technical report on a DEU-modified CLASS/Cobaya validation pipeline for CMB likelihood diagnostics.
The staged workflow constructs a guarded full-objective working lock, runs readiness-cleared DEU and native/baseline chains, and records a matched descriptive DEU/native comparison.
Internal independent-validation estimator diagnostics are reported with caveats, including the DEU/native log-BF-style internal estimator contrast.
The currently locked internal independent-validation log-BF-style value is `169.006408621` for DEU minus native, with DEU log evidence `-542.297479066` and native log evidence `-711.303887687`.
These values are presented as internal estimator results, not as peer-reviewed or final publication claims.
TT_lite remains diagnostic-only and is not included in the sampled production/evidence likelihoods.
