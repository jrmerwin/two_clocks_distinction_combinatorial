This audit fixes the DEU/native cosmological parameters at the lite MAP and mean points, replaces high-l `TTTEEE_lite` with full `TTTEEE`, and optimizes the full-Plik nuisance sector.

Two minimization modes are run:

- `posterior_profile`: maximize posterior / minimize minus-log-posterior, including nuisance priors.
- `likelihood_profile`: maximize likelihood with `ignore_prior: True`, subject to bounds.

## Decision guide

- If both `map` and `mean` nuisance-profiled full-Plik comparisons favor DEU by a large high-l `chi2` gap, the Stage D mean reversal was likely caused by unprofiled nuisance/reference values.
- If the `map` profile favors DEU but the `mean` profile still favors native, the full-Plik posterior geometry is very different from the lite posterior and requires full MCMC.
- If both profiles collapse or favor native, the large lite evidence line should be treated as a `TTTEEE_lite`/nuisance-marginalization artifact.
- Even if the profile favors DEU, this does not solve the restricted-native-baseline issue or the `A_planck` prior issue.
- The next ablation after this is `A_planck = 1` or a broad `A_planck` prior containing unity.
